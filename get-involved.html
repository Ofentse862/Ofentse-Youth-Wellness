Partner With Us

Schools, mental health organizations, and businesses can collaborate with us to expand mental health awareness.

For Schools

  • Free mental health awareness workshops for your learners
  • Access to our online programs and resources
  • Ongoing support for student mental wellness
  • Customized sessions for your school's needs

For Organizations

  • Sponsor mental health workshops
  • Provide mental health resources and expertise
  • Support youth mental wellness initiatives
  • Corporate social responsibility alignment

Support Our Cause

Even if you can't volunteer or partner, you can still help spread mental health awareness.

  • Share our mental health content on social media
  • Connect us with schools in your area
  • Help break the stigma by talking about mental health
  • Donate mental health resources or materials
  • Attend and promote our awareness events
